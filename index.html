Click "Draw" to draw the top card of the deck.

When you have more than 5 cards, click on left or right to view additional cards.

Select cards in your hand using toggle switches.

Click "Discard" to discard all selected cards.

Click "Shuffle" to shuffle the discard pile back into the deck.

Click "Deck" to show the cards in the deck.

Click "Pile" show the cards in the discard pile.

Click "customize" to change the contents of the deck.

Click "instructions" to show or hide this list
